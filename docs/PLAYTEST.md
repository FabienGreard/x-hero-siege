# Playtest

The purpose of this test is to judge the actual game feel, not feature completeness. A clean run should take 5–10 minutes.

## Start

```sh
bun install
bun run check
bun test
bun run dev
```

Open [http://localhost:3000](http://localhost:3000). For co-op checks, open the URL in up to four browser windows.

Useful read-only diagnostics:

- `http://localhost:3000/health`: server health.
- `http://localhost:3000/debug/state`: authoritative room snapshot, including phase, players, hero locks, objective health, and active threats.
- `http://localhost:3000/debug/reset?accelerated=1`: reset into the short visual-QA timeline.
- `http://localhost:3000/debug/advance`: in accelerated mode, advance one authored phase for deterministic visual checks.

## Controls under test

- `WASD`: move
- Mouse: aim
- Hold left mouse: primary attack
- `Q`, `E`, `R`: active abilities
- `F`: ultimate
- `C`: toggle the non-pausing Hero Stats panel
- `B`: browse or close a physical shop while in authoritative range
- `X`: inside an open physical shop, enter or leave exact-slot selling
- `1` / `2`: buy and auto-equip the matching visible ware; at `6/6`, select the incoming ware
- `1`–`6`: while selling or after selecting a reforge ware, choose the exact occupied socket
- `Enter`: confirm the reviewed sale or reforge; `Escape` backs out one trade step without spending
- Click the gold `+` on an ability slot: spend a skill point directly from the action bar
- `Ctrl` + `Q`, `E`, `R`, or `F`: matching keyboard upgrade shortcut

## Golden-path run

1. Select one of the four heroes and start the run.
2. Confirm the Aegis Citadel, central Heartfire Nexus, four gates, and current threat direction are understandable without explanation. Every active City Watch tile must expose the exact gate percentage and restrained fill, every sealed approach must remain `SEALED`, and the nearby physical gate must carry the same subordinate health read without competing with the Nexus bar.
3. Move, aim independently, attack while repositioning, and use every learned ability once.
4. Fight the opening wave and note whether common enemies die quickly with readable impact feedback.
5. Spend each earned skill point from the action bar without pausing the fight. Confirm the `+` disappears, the rank pip fills, and clicking the learned slot still casts normally.
6. Earn at least 60 gold. Confirm the local gold tile changes from `GOLD` to `WARE READY` and both existing shape-distinct shop marks receive equal restrained minimap outlines. Press remote `B` and confirm no panel opens; the cue means funded, not safe or globally accessible. Use exact gate durability together with the real outbound pressure to create a safe retreat window rather than shopping while enemies are already striking the gate.
7. Visit the northwest Ironbound Forge. Confirm its warm landmark, minimap marker, proximity prompt, and travel cost are readable; open it with `B` and verify each ordinary card costs `60` and names the exact next Basic Damage or Move Speed result before spending. Buy by mouse or `1`/`2`, reconcile that preview with the accepted Hero Stats change while enemies continue acting, and confirm a rapid double-click cannot buy twice against one result. On acceptance, the incoming ware's established shape and color must flash once on the local hero while one world label and the existing toast repeat the exact incoming stat result; snapshot history and allies must not replay that ordinary receipt. The build summary must name the ware, show `×1`, expose its canonical total effect, and read `ATTUNEMENT 1/4` both there and on its local shop card. After buying, the hero must wear that ware's matching shape-coded signature in the world. Press `X`, select that exact slot by mouse and by its number in separate attempts, and confirm the sale preview names `+30` gold, the open slot, stack, Hero Stat, signature, and any threshold loss; press `Escape` and verify nothing changes.
8. Re-establish the lane, earn another 60 gold, and create a second safe window before visiting the northeast Veilglass Reliquary. Confirm its cool shrine and marker cannot be confused with the Forge; verify the ordinary cards name the exact next Skill Power or Cooldown Speed result even before the wallet can afford them, then buy one ware and reconcile the accepted world receipt with the `ABILITY IMPACT` block. Every learned champion ability must name its current rank, exact per-target magnitude, and effective cooldown; unlearned casts must remain unlearned. Confirm Focus changes only real ability magnitudes, Quickening changes only `Q`/`E`/`R`/`F` recovery without changing LMB cadence, and the block fits beside the open shop without hiding the battlefield or action bar. Each shop card's owned count must reconcile with the six visible sockets, while the dominant signature must follow copy count and first-slot tie-breaking.
9. Leave each vendor's range and confirm the panel closes. Press `B` remotely, then enter the other vendor's range and reopen it; only that physical shop's two wares and their current owned counts should appear.
10. Build one ware to `×2` and confirm Hero Stats and its local shop card both advance to `ATTUNEMENT 2/4`; build a non-Greaves ware to `×3` and confirm both say `NEXT ATTUNES` while the card shows the exact nonlinear next Hero Stat result. For Greaves `×3`, confirm the same threshold becomes the more specific `UNLOCKS COMBAT STRIDE`, and the fourth-copy card shows both the exact Move Speed step and the current champion's exact `WORLD/S DURING LMB`. Then buy or reforge a fourth copy. When the loadout is full, the slot-specific preview must show `×3 → ×4 ATTUNED`, the doubled fourth-copy stat step, and any gained or lost Combat Stride rate. After acceptance, Hero Stats must show the raw `×4`, its effective total, and `COMBAT STRIDE`; the named `LMB` row must show the same rate. Hold movement and primary together: Attuned Greaves must retain `15%` of current Move Speed during windup and impact, recovery must remain `45%`, idle must remain full speed, and abilities must gain no Combat Stride movement. Exactly once, the existing ware shape should bloom around the purchasing hero with a short local stinger and exact count toast, then settle into a faint second echo behind the hero. An ally should see a quieter world flare without receiving the buyer's toast or audio. A fifth and sixth copy must preview their exact higher stride rates without replaying the ceremony. Reforge back to `×3` and confirm both Combat Stride and the old echo release quietly. Compare a `3/3` breadth build with a `4/2` specialist build and confirm the choice is useful rather than compulsory.
11. Fill all six sockets, confirm the readiness label changes to `REFORGE READY` only when the wallet can fund a legal `30`-gold net trade, then test both ways the Armory buys back value. First press `X`, choose one occupied socket by mouse or `1`–`6`, and confirm the final sale review names the exact outgoing effect, stack and Attunement change, every changed Hero Stat, resulting dominant signature, `+30` wallet result, and open slot. Confirm once: gold rises by exactly 30, only that slot clears, a direct receipt plays once, and reconnect preserves the accepted wallet and hole. Refill the open slot through an ordinary `60`-gold purchase. Then earn a fresh 30 gold if needed and reshape the full build atomically: the selected card must read `30 NET`, and final confirmation must state `BUY 60 − TRADE-IN 30 = 30 GOLD NET`. If one local ware already fills all six sockets, confirm its card says disabled `FULL STACK`; mouse and its `1`/`2` shortcut must announce that the build cannot change without opening a socket picker, sending a request, or spending gold. For every legal reforge, select the incoming ware by mouse or `1`/`2`, select an occupied socket by mouse or `1`–`6`, and reconcile the preview with the authoritative result: gold falls by exactly 30, the build never exposes an intermediate hole, the chosen stat and grouped totals update, both affected shop-owned counts remain truthful, Attunement is gained or lost only at four raw copies, the dominant world signature changes only when socket leadership or Attunement changes, and the build remains `6/6`. A build with five matching copies and one different ware must still allow that one socket to become the sixth matching copy. Back out once with `Escape` and confirm no gold is spent.
12. Respond to the forced gate breach. Confirm the active gate moves calmly through healthy, pressured, and critical reads before settling on `FALLEN`; pressure must clearly shift toward protecting the Nexus, and the fallen gate must not imply that it ended the run.
13. Defeat the siege threat, follow the opened counterattack route as a group, and destroy the Rift source.
14. Confirm the victory payoff is unambiguous and the run ends cleanly.

Repeat once while allowing enemies to reach and destroy the Nexus. Confirm defeat occurs because Nexus health reaches zero, not merely because a gate falls.

## Hero checks

Run at least the opening combat with each hero:

- **Warden:** melee control and frontline durability read clearly.
- **Riftstalker:** ranged precision and mobility read clearly.
- **Ashcaller:** placed fire and explosive area damage read clearly.
- **Gravebinder:** horde-derived sustain and spirits read clearly.

Each hero must be capable of solo wave clear and boss damage. Complementary co-op effects are welcome; dependency on a healer, tank, or specific composition is a failure.

## Multiplayer checks

1. Connect two clients and attempt to reserve the same hero at nearly the same time. Exactly one request must succeed.
2. Connect four clients. Lock all four distinct heroes and confirm the fifth-player limit is not exceeded.
3. Move and attack from every client. Confirm all clients observe the same players, enemies, objective health, phase, and outcome.
4. Split across approaches during defense, then regroup for the counterattack. Confirm threat and teammate readability at both scales.
5. Confirm XP/power progression is shared fairly and no player can steal another player's required rewards.
6. Disconnect one equipped defender during active play. Confirm every peer retains exactly one inert `RECONNECTING` reservation, then reload within 15 seconds and verify the same player ID, hero, wallet, six-slot build, stats, ranks, cooldowns, host role, and immediate next input return without a fifth player or replayed progression feedback.

## Feel checklist

- Input feels immediate; no obvious latency between aim, attack, cast, and impact.
- The camera never hides the hero, Nexus, or critical telegraphs.
- Enemy density creates momentum without obscuring dangerous attacks.
- Hits have visible and audible weight without excessive shake or particle clutter.
- The HUD answers: who am I, what is ready, where is pressure, how long can each active gate hold, and how close is the Nexus to defeat?
- Each ability rank creates a noticeable build change rather than a hidden numerical bump.
- The breach is the run's pressure peak.
- The counterattack is short, collective, and more aggressive than the defense.
- Victory and defeat are both clear.

## Record after each run

- Player count and selected heroes.
- Total run time.
- Victory or defeat and the phase where it occurred.
- Most satisfying moment.
- Most confusing or visually noisy moment.
- Any input, networking, or frame-rate issue.
- One change that would most improve the next playtest.

After verifying the slice, record the result and begin the next cycle from the newly verified build. Do not use playtest observations as permission to add deferred systems or change the approved game promise.
